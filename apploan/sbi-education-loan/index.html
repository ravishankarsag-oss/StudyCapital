SBI Scholar Loan Scheme — Best for IIT, IIM, NIT Students

🏛️

Premier Institutes Only

IIT, IIM, NIT, AIIMS, NLU, IISc, XLRI, NIFT, IHM, and other Category A institutions. SBI maintains an approved list — StudyCapital confirms if your institution qualifies.

🔓

No Collateral — Up to ₹40 Lakhs

Unlike the standard Student Loan (₹7.5L no collateral), Scholar Loan gives ₹40 lakhs without any security — purely on institutional reputation and co-applicant income.

Lowest Rate in India

At 8.5%–10.0% p.a. (with female concession at 8.0%–9.5%), SBI Scholar Loan is the cheapest education loan in India for eligible students.
