Requirements

Education Loan Eligibility & Documents Required

✅ Eligibility Criteria

  • ✓Indian national, aged 18–35 years
  • ✓Secured admission in a recognised institution (India or abroad)
  • ✓Minimum 60% marks in qualifying examination
  • ✓Co-applicant (parent/guardian) with stable, documented income
  • ✓No existing default on any previous education loan
  • ✓Collateral may be required for loans above ₹7.5 lakhs

📋 Documents Required

  • ✓Admission letter / offer letter from institution
  • ✓10th, 12th, and graduation marksheets
  • ✓Co-applicant KYC: Aadhaar, PAN, Passport
  • ✓Income proof: salary slips (3 months) or ITR (2 years)
  • ✓Bank statements of co-applicant (last 6 months)
  • ✓Fee structure / cost of attendance certificate
  • ✓Collateral documents if applicable (property papers, FD certs)
