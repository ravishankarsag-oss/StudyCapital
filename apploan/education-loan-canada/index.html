GIC Explained

What Is GIC for Canada? Everything You Need to Know

🏦

What Is a GIC?

A Guaranteed Investment Certificate is a fixed deposit of CAD 10,000 in an approved Canadian bank (Scotiabank, CIBC, HSBC Canada). It proves to IRCC that you can support yourself during your first year in Canada.

💡

Who Requires It?

Students applying for a Canadian study permit under the Student Direct Stream (SDS) must submit a GIC. Non-SDS students also benefit as it strengthens their visa application.
