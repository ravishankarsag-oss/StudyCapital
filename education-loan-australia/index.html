Which Courses & Universities Are Covered?

Eligible courses: Masters, Graduate Diploma, MBA, Nursing

Popular universities: University of Melbourne, ANU, UNSW, University of Sydney, Monash, UQ, and 500+ more recognised institutions in Australia.

Documents Required for Education Loan

  • 📄 Admission letter / Offer letter from university
  • 📄 Academic marksheets — 10th, 12th, and graduation
  • 📄 Co-applicant KYC: Aadhaar, PAN Card, Passport
  • 📄 Income proof: Latest 3 salary slips OR 2 years ITR
  • 📄 Bank statements (last 6 months)
  • 📄 Fee structure / Cost of education certificate from university
  • 📄 Collateral documents if applicable (property papers, FD certificate)
  • 📄 OSHC (Overseas Student Health Cover) is mandatory and included in the loan documents (arranged by StudyCapital)
