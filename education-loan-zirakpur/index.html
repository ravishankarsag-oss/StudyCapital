Canada Focus

Canada Study Loans & GIC — Complete Assistance for Punjab Students

Punjab sends more students to Canada than any other Indian state. At StudyCapital Zirakpur, we've built a specialised end-to-end process for Canada-bound students that covers every requirement:

💳

Education Loan for Canada

Loans up to ₹75 lakhs for Canadian universities covering tuition, accommodation, airfare, health insurance and GIC. Banks: SBI, HDFC Credila, Axis Bank, ICICI Bank.

🇨🇦

GIC Arrangement

Canada requires a Guaranteed Investment Certificate (GIC) of CAD 10,000 (~₹6.2 lakhs) for study permit. We arrange GIC through approved Canadian banks — faster than doing it yourself.

💱

Forex Remittance

We assist with remitting tuition fees and GIC amount to Canada in CAD at competitive exchange rates — through RBI-authorised forex partners.
